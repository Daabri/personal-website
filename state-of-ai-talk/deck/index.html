// What I'd change here

So, what we
do with this.

  • Put the tools in everyone's hands, on our real data and code
  • Write down what we repeat, as skills the whole team reuses
  • Build one MCP doorway into our tools, so every team plugs in
  • Let loops handle the repeatable work, with limits and checks
  • Then open that doorway to our partners
// The takeaway
The tools keep changing. What lasts is our connected knowledge, and the judgment behind it.

We are not chasing models. We are building the door.
